Privacy Policy

Data Collection Policy

This website does not collect any personal data from visitors. We do not use cookies, analytics, or any other tracking technologies on this site.

GitHub Pages Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect some basic information about visitors as part of its standard hosting services. This information is not accessible to us and is solely managed by GitHub according to their privacy policies.

For more information about how GitHub handles your data, please refer to the

GitHub General Privacy Statement.

Contact Form

The contact form on this website uses your default email client to send messages. No data from the contact form is stored on our servers or transmitted to any third-party services.

Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure of your personal data
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Since this website does not collect or process personal data, these rights are not applicable in this context. However, you may have these rights in relation to GitHub's data processing as the hosting provider.

Data Protection Officer

As this website does not process personal data, we have not appointed a Data Protection Officer. For any privacy-related inquiries, you can contact me directly via the contact form on this website.

Third-Party Resources

This website uses third-party resources to enhance functionality and appearance. When you visit this website, requests may be made to the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: Used to load custom fonts. For more information, please see the Google Fonts Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare CDN: Used to load Font Awesome icons. For more information, please see the Cloudflare Privacy Policy and CDNJS Cloudflare Policy.
  • GitHub: Used to load various icons from raw.githubusercontent.com. For more information, please see the GitHub General Privacy Statement.

These third-party services may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, and the page you are visiting. This information is subject to their respective privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
